About CropConnect

Empowering Farmers. Connecting Consumers.

Challenges in Indian Agriculture

Middlemen Exploitation

Farmers' incomes reduced by up to 60% due to intermediaries.

Limited Market Access

Small and marginal farmers lack direct access to markets.

Inflated Consumer Prices

Consumers face higher prices due to supply chain inefficiencies.

Lack of Transparent Crop Exchange

Opaque systems hinder fair trade between farmers and buyers.

Post-Harvest Loss

Significant produce wasted due to inefficient supply chains.

Our Solution

CropConnect is a web-based platform connecting farmers directly with buyers, eliminating intermediaries.

  • Farmer Empowerment: Farmers can list produce, set prices, and negotiate directly with buyers.
  • Buyer Access: Buyers browse a variety of crops with transparent pricing and real-time availability.
  • Transparent Transactions: Direct interactions ensure fair trade and build trust.

Platform Features

Farmer Crop Listing

Farmers can log in and list their crops with name, quantity, price, and photos.

Buyer Crop Marketplace

Buyers can browse, filter, and search for crops by category, location, or price.

Role-Based Login System

Separate dashboards and access control for Farmers and Buyers using secure authentication (JWT).

Trust Score System

After each transaction, buyers rate sellers, building a trust-based marketplace and highlighting top-rated farmers.

Order Placement & History

Buyers can place orders, and both parties can view current and past orders in their dashboard.

Mobile-Responsive UI

Clean and responsive design using Bootstrap, ensuring accessibility across devices.

AI & ML Integrations

Predictive Crop Price Forecasting

Utilizes LSTM models to predict future crop prices based on historical data, weather patterns, and market trends.

AI-Powered Crop Quality Assessment

Employs CNNs to automatically classify and grade crops based on quality through image analysis.

Fraud Prevention

Utilizes anomaly detection models to identify suspicious activities like price manipulation or fraudulent transactions.

Personalized Recommendations

Implements recommendation systems to suggest relevant crops to buyers and offer farming tips to sellers.

Seller Trust Validation Score

Calculates trust scores for sellers based on the amount of product sold and buyer ratings.
